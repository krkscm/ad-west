# Coverage Definition and Criteria

## Coverage Goal
100 percent planning-document coverage means each business intake requirement, constraint, deferral, and delivery commitment is mapped to:
- at least one SDLC artifact,
- at least one acceptance checkpoint,
- a review/signoff owner,
- and a tracking status.

## Coverage Dimensions
1. Functional Scope Coverage.
2. Non-Functional Coverage.
3. Constraint Coverage.
4. Deferral Control Coverage.
5. Data Model Coverage.
6. Delivery Timeline Coverage.
7. Risk and Compliance Coverage.
8. Governance and Reporting Coverage.

## Completion Criteria
- No intake item remains unmapped.
- No MVP feature lacks acceptance criteria.
- No deferred item lacks explicit exclusion control.
- Signoff checklist is completed with approver names and dates.

## Review Owners
- Business completeness: Product Owner.
- Technical completeness: Tech Lead.
- Delivery completeness: Project Manager.
- Final submission approval: Sponsor.
