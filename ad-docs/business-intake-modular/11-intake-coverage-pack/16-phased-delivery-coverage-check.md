# Phased Delivery Coverage Check

## Intake Delivery Commitment
- 16-week plan with Sprint 1 through Sprint 8.

## Sprint Coverage
- Sprint 1-2: foundation and authentication.
- Sprint 3-4: contact core, import, dedup.
- Sprint 5-6: programs and attendance.
- Sprint 7: self-service and credential+captcha workflows.
- Sprint 8: helpdesk, notifications, wrap-up and readiness.

## Verification Checklist
- [ ] Sprint objectives are documented.
- [ ] Dependencies and risks are tracked.
- [ ] Milestone evidence is recorded.
- [ ] Forecast updates are reflected in reporting.
