# Executive One-Page Plan Summary

## Problem
Member records are fragmented across Srenies, causing operational inefficiency and duplicate data.

## Core Business Decision
Deliver the minimum platform scope that resolves contact fragmentation first, then supports governance and operations.

## Core Business In Scope
- Modules 1-8 only.
- Password+captcha authentication with no messaging workflows in current phase.
- Fixed three-role RBAC model.
- Manual import dedup process using phone and email rules.

## Delivery Plan
- 16 weeks across 8 sprint blocks.
- Team size 2 to 3.
- Foundation -> Contact Core -> Program/Attendance -> Self-Service -> Helpdesk/Wrap-up.

## Risk Position
Primary risks are data quality, dependency delays, governance complexity, and compliance obligations.
Mitigation is embedded in dedup-first flow, simplified workflows, and compliance controls.

## Governance Ask
Approve this plan baseline, enforce deferred-scope controls, and proceed with phased delivery governance.
