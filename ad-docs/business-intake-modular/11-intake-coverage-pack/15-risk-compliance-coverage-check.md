# Risk and Compliance Coverage Check

## Intake Risk Themes
- Poor quality imported Sreny data.
- WhatsApp procurement and external dependency risk.
- Governing body annual rotation UX complexity.
- GDPR and UAE data law obligations.

## Coverage Controls
- Import validation and dedup-first priority.
- Email-first notifications in MVP.
- Simplified governance yearly rollover process.
- Soft-delete and data-erasure process in requirements and controls.

## Verification Checklist
- [ ] Risks logged in risk register.
- [ ] Mitigations mapped to implementation and test plans.
- [ ] Compliance evidence tracked in security/compliance phase docs.
- [ ] Residual risk signoff captured.
